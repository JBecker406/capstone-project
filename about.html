Who We Are

Solem Avium is a solar panel manufacturing and installation company founded by Christopher Garcia, Peter Tran, and Jeremy Becker. The name of the company is Latin for Sun Birds since the company was founded and is located in Phoenix. The company provides a cheaper source of obtaining solar panels in order to make more of an impact with solar energy.

Christopher Garcia

Christopher is the main businessman and organized a business plan to get the company off the ground.

Peter Tran

Peter is the main PR official and created the presentation to start the company.

Jeremy Becker

Jeremy is the head developer for the website and is responsible for the majority of the site.

Our goal for the company

The company's main goal is to achieve reduced prices for energy, as well as a decreased impact on the environment. We hope to provide communities with cheaper solar panels so that they can produce their own, clean energy.
